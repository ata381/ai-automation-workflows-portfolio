# 📧 Email Summary Assistant

## The Real-World Problem
As a busy entrepreneur or small business owner, you probably spend hours every day reading through customer emails. Some are short and clear, but many are long, rambling messages that take forever to understand.

**Sound familiar?**
- "Can you please read this 300-word email and tell me what they actually want?"
- "I don't have time to go through all these support emails!"
- "How do I know which customer issues are urgent?"

## Our AI Solution
We built an intelligent email assistant that reads your customer emails and gives you exactly what you need to respond quickly and effectively.

**What it does:**
- **Quick Summary:** Turns long emails into one clear sentence about what the customer wants
- **Urgency Assessment:** Tells you if this needs immediate attention (high), can wait a bit (medium), or isn't urgent (low)
- **Action Guidance:** Suggests exactly what you should do next to solve their problem

## Example in Action
**Input Email:**
```
Subject: Account Access Issue

Hi team,

I've been trying to log into my account for the past two days but keep getting an error message. I need access urgently as I have a deadline tomorrow. Can you please help resolve this as soon as possible?

Thanks,
Alex
```

**AI Output:**
- **Summary:** Customer cannot access their account and has an urgent deadline
- **Urgency:** High (needs immediate attention)
- **Suggested Action:** Prioritize and resolve the login issue immediately

## Business Benefits
- **Save 5 minutes per email** – Get key information instantly instead of reading everything

- **Respond faster** – Know exactly what action to take without second-guessing
- **Scale your business** – Handle more customers without feeling overwhelmed

## How to Try It
This demo works completely offline – no internet or API keys needed!

1. Open your terminal/command prompt
2. Run: `python email_summary_workflow.py`
3. Choose to use our sample email or paste your own
4. See how AI summarizes and prioritizes the email instantly
